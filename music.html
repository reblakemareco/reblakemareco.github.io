Music Projects

Luck Penny

I play Irish traditional music on flute and whistles.  As Luck Penny, I have performed with various other musicians throughout Louisiana, Mississippi, and other locations.  Active from 2012 - 2015.  

Handsome Molly

Irish traditional music and song performed by Rachael Blake on flute and whistles, Kelly Kennedy on vocals and piano, Andy Cleveland on fiddle, and Craig DuBose on guitar and vocals.  Together they performed throughout Virginia, Louisiana, and other locations and were active from 2010 - 2014.  

Baroque Flute

Beginning study at Oberlin College, I honed my baroque flute skills through workshops and performance opportunities in various localities.  I continue to pursue opportunities to play this music whenever possible.    

Early Music Ensemble - College of William & Mary

I performed music from the Renaissance through the Baroque on flutes and recorders, in collaboration with faculty and students.   This ensemble produced the above limited edition recording including Bach's Brandenburg Concerto No. 5 and selections from A Musical Offering.
